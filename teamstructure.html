Team Structure

📋 Recruitment

Open tryouts and school announcements to find passionate new members.

📚 Learning

Teaching new members about FIRST, robot design, and team culture.

🎓 Instruction

Hands-on workshops where members learn technical skills in hardware, software, and CAD.

🎉 Community

Team bonding events, social activities, and building a supportive community.

Team Roles

Every member chooses a specialty (Hardware, Software, CAD, Outreach). Everyone learns a bit of everything for true teamwork.

Captains are picked by democratic vote after speeches. Two co-captains help balance leadership.
