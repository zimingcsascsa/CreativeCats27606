Team Structure

Recruitment

Open tryouts and school announcements to find passionate new members.

Learning

Teaching new members about FIRST, robot design, and team culture.

Instruction

Hands-on workshops for hardware, software, and CAD skills.

FUN

Team bonding events and building a supportive community.

Team Roles

Every member chooses a specialty (Hardware, Software, CAD, Outreach). Everyone learns a bit of everything for true teamwork.

Captains are picked by democratic vote after speeches. Two co-captains help balance leadership.

Team Plan

Season Overview

  • Kickoff: Introduce new members and review rules
  • Prototyping and brainstorming (multiple designs tested)
  • Build sessions and programming sprints
  • Outreach events/robot demos in schools/community
  • Competitions: Scrimmages, Leagues, State
  • Review, reflect, and prepare for next season

Goals

  • Consistent autonomous scoring
  • 100+ outreach hours
  • Mentor 5+ rookie teams
  • Document and improve robot reliability
  • Grow social media presence
