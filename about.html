About Us

How we work, what we believe, and where we're headed.

Recruitment

Open tryouts and school announcements. We look for people who are curious, not just experienced.

Learning

New members learn about FIRST, robot design, and how we work as a team before touching hardware.

Instruction

Hands-on workshops for hardware, software, and CAD. Everyone learns the basics of everything.

FUN

Team bonding events and building a community. If it's not fun, what's the point?

Season Plan

  • Kickoff — introduce new members, review rules
  • Prototyping — test multiple designs before committing
  • Build sessions and programming sprints
  • Outreach events and robot demos
  • Competitions — scrimmages, leagues, state
  • Review, reflect, prep for next season

Goals

  • Consistent autonomous scoring
  • 100+ outreach hours
  • Mentor 5+ rookie teams
  • Document and improve robot reliability
  • Grow social media presence

Every member picks a specialty — Hardware, Software, CAD, or Outreach. But everyone learns a bit of everything. That's how real teams work.

Captains are elected by democratic vote after giving speeches. Two co-captains share the load so no one person burns out.

Team Plan

We meet once a week (sometimes more before competitions). Each session has a clear agenda — build, code, test, or outreach. We document everything in meeting logs.
