Sponsor Us

Help a small team do big things. Every dollar goes directly to parts, competition fees, and outreach.

We're a small, underfunded FTC team from Austin, Texas. We don't have a big budget or corporate backing — just students who want to build robots and learn engineering. Your sponsorship directly funds:

  • Robot parts and components (motors, servos, electronics)
  • Competition registration fees
  • Tools and prototyping materials
  • Outreach events and workshops for younger students

In return, we put your name on our robot, our website, and our competition jerseys. We also post about our sponsors on social media and mention you at every event.
