Who We Are

We're high school students from Austin who like building robots and figuring things out. Some of us code, some of us wrench, some of us do both badly — but we're learning.

Team 27606 competes in FIRST Tech Challenge. We're a small team and are extremely underfunded, but what makes us unique is that our team members come from different schools across Austin. We unite around our passion for robotics and STEM.
