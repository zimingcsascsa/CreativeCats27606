About Us

We're high school students developing robots, documenting our work, and promoting STEM through outreach and competition.

Since our founding, Team 27606 has competed in the FIRST Tech Challenge, focusing on building, programming, and documenting robots that solve complex engineering challenges.

Proud to be a small, underfunded FTC team dedicated to learning, competing, and helping others discover robotics and STEM.
