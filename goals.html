Meet Reflections

Meeting What went well What didn't Improvements
LM1 Had a robot that moved! Communicated with alliance. No auton, inaccurate bot, no driving practice, lack of experience. Create color-sensing auton, improve accuracy, build V2.
LM2 Had an auton! Accuracy improved. Clear team roles. No intake, lots of backspin, couldn't defend against ramming. Add defense, finish V2 CAD, more driver practice.
LM3 Prototyped intake, auton worked reliably, played defense well. Couldn't finish intake, couldn't block heavy robots, long auton didn't shoot. Finish intake, prototype V2, driver practice with intake.
